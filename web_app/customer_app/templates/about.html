About

Online shopping has become a big part of our lives. It has broadened commerce as people can buy products from anywhere at anytime. However, trade is built on customer-seller interactions. Most online shopping platform lack such interactivity features. Soko aims to solve this by providing real time communication between customers and sellers. Customers can:

  1. Make more informed decision when buying products
  2. Track their oder fulfillment process
  3. Get updated of new products and restocking of out of stock products
  4. Gain more trust in the online shopping

Soko also offers other features, including:"

  1. An easily accessible shopping cart to resolve common cart abandonment issues
  2. Instant checkout with the Buy Now option
  3. Updated order tracking through in-app and email notification-options
  4. Saving products for feature purchase

This project was inspired by the need to solve the above challenges of contemporary online shopping platform. The code for this project can be found on GitHub.
